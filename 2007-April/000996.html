Comment (by bonefish):

 Sorry, I can't help you, if you don't provide further infos. The
 build_cross_tools_gcc4 script passes "--target=..." to the binutils
 configure (cf. line 83). If the "=" gets lost, I'd blame your
 configuration. The shebang of our script, that of binutils' configure and
 config.sub is "#!/bin/sh", which should cause something UNIX shell
 compatible to be chosen as interpreter. If not, then your configuration is
 broken.
