Comment (by wkornewald):

 You can escape GetMouse by putting a "!" in front: !GetMouse

 The search field at the top acts as a quickjump if it detects a certain
 pattern (you can also enter revision numbers like r123 or ticket numbers
 like #1161 there). In this case, Trac recognizes a wiki word and tries to
 bring you to the wiki page. In order to work around this, you can enter
 !GetMouse ("!" at the beginning disables the quickjump when searching).

 Is that good enough? It can only be disabled by hacking the Trac source,
 but it might be a one-liner, so not too difficult. OTOH, the less
 modifications we have the better. And the quickjump can be very handy. :)
