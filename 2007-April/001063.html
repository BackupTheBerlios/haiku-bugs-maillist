New description:

 PowerPC support should arrive after R1, the PS3 could be an ideal target
 for Haiku due to its multi-threaded architecture, ie. translators and
 libraries could be optimized to use the Cell, giving the whole OS and
 applications that use the optimized components a performance boost.

 Areas that need looking into are:

  * How does the boot process work
  * What devices are presented to an OS
  * What components of Haiku can be optimized to use the Cell BBE's SPU's
  * Is the memory layout to restrictive to make a port whorthwile

 ''Note to anyone reading this - don't get excited - this isn't someone
 working on a PS3 port.  Its not news.  Nothing may come out of this.''
