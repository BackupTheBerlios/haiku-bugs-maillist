 Clean unix apps would just use:
 {{{
 AC_CHECK_HEADER(sys/poll.h)
 AC_CHECK_HEADER(poll.h)
 }}}
 and:
 {{{
 #ifdef HAVE_SYS_POLL_H
 #include <sys/poll.h>
 #else
 #ifdef HAVE_POLL_H
 #include <poll.h>
 #else
 #error missing poll.h
 #endif
 #endif
 }}}
 Others aren't worth porting anyway.
