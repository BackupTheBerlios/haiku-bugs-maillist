 - Start the Workspaces app.
 - Open a window, e.g. start a Terminal.
 - Change to another workspace.
 - Select "Hide all" of the app (here:Terminal) from the Deskbar.

 -> Workspaces only redraws the inactive workspace after a mouse click. It
 should be immediately, just like when you do the same thing while being in
 the same workspace as the app (here:Terminal).
