 Hi.
  In the 18th May 2007 verion of Haiku, when you set the time uing the
 Preferences/Time app, the time given (for Auckland/NZ) is two hours too
 early. For example, if the time for Auckland/NZ appears as 9.05am, it
 should actually be 11.05am.

 As an aside, if you want to also set "daylight saving" time correctly for
 New Zealand, the Government here recently extended the period that it
 covers. It is now as follows -

 a) It will now start on the last Sunday in September (add 1 hour).
 b) It will now end on the first Sunday in April (subtract 1 hour).
