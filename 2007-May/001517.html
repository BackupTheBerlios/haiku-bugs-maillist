 Now I see it: this is a USB 1.1 device and you are stuck with OHCI.
 Sorry, but there is no other "fix" than to implement OHCI in this case.
 It is not possible for EHCI to communicate with these devices. If you
 simply force keeping the ownership you at most risk damaging the device,
 but you will certainly not get it working. EHCI uses a different protocol
 which is incompatible with the one of older devices.
 I will close this bug as a duplicate, as bug #1044 already covers this.
