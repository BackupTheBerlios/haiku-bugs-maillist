 The partition list of Haiku's bootman differs from BeOS's bootman.

 First of all, only the partitions on the first harddisk are listed.
 Apparently B_GET_BIOS_DRIVE_ID isn't implemented yet, see [http://dev
 .haiku-os.org/changeset/25044 Changeset 25044].

 Secondly, the Intel Extended Partition is listed which isn't bootable as
 far as I know.
