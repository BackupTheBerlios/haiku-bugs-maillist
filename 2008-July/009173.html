Comment(by stippi):

 The problem seems to be that app_server and the graphics driver are out of
 sync with regards to the current resolution. In another words, the
 app_server thinks that the driver is in another resolution than it
 actually is. Then it writes to the frame buffer out of bounds which leads
 to the crash. From what I hear from mmlr, the problem can be worked around
 by copying a resolution settings file which forces an initial mode switch
 and syncs app_server to the driver or vice versa. (mmlr, does a vesa
 settings file suffice?) I have not yet had a chance to look into the root
 cause of the problem.
