Comment:

 I have found the problem in the Radeon driver. Because it depends on which
 link you chose to plug in your screen, it also explains why it worked for
 Marcus and not for me. The repeated screen contents on the right side are
 an effect of how the Radeon driver handles non-native resolutions. It
 drives the screen at its native resolution, I think because it thinks the
 screen is a laptop panel that has no other capabilities, and then does
 nothing about the extra space (TODO in the code mentions there would be
 garbage there). Fixed in r26540.
