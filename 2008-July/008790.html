 When I select partial text from a text entry box, the text moves down by
 one pixel and then up by one pixel as long as I leave my left mouse button
 down.  Wasn't sure if this was a bug or if it was supposed to be that way.
 To check it, hit alt-f then type something in the find box, and then
 highlight some of the text with your mouse and keep the button down, the
 text will now be bouncing up and down.
