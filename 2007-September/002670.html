 Great feature, but it responds over a larger area than DeskbAct, thus
 causing a greater risk of irritation through unwanted activation when
 cursors accidentaly hit the side of the screen. This area can get quite
 large (vertically) when many apps are opened and Deskbar isn't in its
 minimized mode.
 I'd love to see the responding area reduced to a point, i.e. the exact
 corner, just like in BeOS' DeskbAct app.
