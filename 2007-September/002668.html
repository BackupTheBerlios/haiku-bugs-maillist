 In BeOS, the mouse speed and acceleration can effectively be set on and
 anywhere between the markers.
 In Haiku this can be done too, but without lasting effect: as soon as the
 'Mouse' window is closed after choosing the settings, the sliders move to
 the nearest marker and their speed/acceleration is used.
 This means there are only 7 settings for Speed and 5 settings for
 Acceleration in Haiku.
