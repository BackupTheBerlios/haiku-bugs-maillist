 The following line is recorded in a BPicture:

 BRect frame(0, 0, 100, 30);
 view->StrokeLine(BPoint(frame.left, frame.top), BPoint(frame.right,
 frame.bottom));

 The picture is then drawn into a bitmap. Then the flattened and
 unflattened picture is also drawn into another bitmap.
 The two bitmaps are not pixel identical.

 See FlattenPictureTest test application in
 src/tests/kits/interface/unflatten_picture/PictureTestCases.cpp
 http://svn.berlios.de/viewcvs/haiku/haiku/trunk/src/tests/kits/interface/flatten_picture/PictureTestCases.cpp?rev=21888&view=markup
