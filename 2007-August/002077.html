#1358: disallow signature in comments
---------------------------+------------------------------------------------
 Reporter:  jonas.kirilla  |       Owner:  gavin         
     Type:  enhancement    |      Status:  new           
 Priority:  normal         |   Milestone:  R1            
Component:  - Website/CMS  |     Version:  R1 development
 Platform:  All            |  
---------------------------+------------------------------------------------
 The use of lines or blocks of "signature" in comments, on the website, as
 allowed/suggested by the user profile form, mixes relevant content (the
 discussion) with irrelevant content (the signature). It's a nuisance, to
 me. Some small amount of time and mental resources is wasted reading each
 sig line, trying to figure out if its part of the comment (to be read) or
 not part of the comment (to be skipped). The user name and profile link
 should be enough for everyone. No signatures and, please, no images. I'm
 hoping this misfeature can be disabled, either globally, or by user-
 preference (hiding other people's signature).
