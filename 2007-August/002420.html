Comment (by axeld):

 Thanks! Since a few revisions, the signal handlers are cleared in exec() -
 that weren't done before. Quoting the description of sigaction() from the
 Open Group Base Specifications:

 "Once an action is installed for a specific signal, it shall remain
 installed until another action is explicitly requested (by another call to
 sigaction()), until the SA_RESETHAND flag causes resetting of the handler,
 or until one of the exec functions is called."

 Since this ticket has been reopened, maybe the reappearance was caused by
 this change. That would at least justify to open a new ticket, as the
 original problem was indeed fixed.
