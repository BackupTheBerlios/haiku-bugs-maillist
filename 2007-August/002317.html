Comment:

 I've found the problem: when executing the application, some pages are
 moved from the modified list to the active list - therefore, when the
 cache is written back, those pages are simply not written. On next boot,
 those pages are just empty (or just contain garbage, since BFS currently
 does not clear unused space).
