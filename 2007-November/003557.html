 First of all, The "always on top" option on Deskbar settings is OFF.

 Now try doing this. Open a window of any kind (I tried Home folder and

 Slowly drag the window over Deskbar, notice how everything works fine.

 1. Instead of having the window floating on top, it gets below the deskbar
