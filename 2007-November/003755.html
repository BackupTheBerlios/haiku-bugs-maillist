 Replying to [comment:6 axeld]:
 > We shouldn't have to clear the TLS slots since they are put into a clean
 area (as part of the thread's stack). If they are dirty, some code uses
 this part early on, and that shouldn't happen.

 Please have a look at arch_thread_init_tls() -- the TLS data are copied
 from a mostly uninitialized array on the stack. A memset() there will do
 the trick, but the pthreads issue should probably be looked into first,
 before it is hidden.
