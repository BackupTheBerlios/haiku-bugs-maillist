Comment(by Adek336):

 As for syslog losing messages: it happens only if the via_rhine driver is
 available at boot-time. So, delete the driver (/boot/beos/system/add-
 ons/kernel/drivers/bin/via_rhine and the /boot/home/config/... equivalent)
 and boot without it. After boot place the driver file where it should be
 and, voila. Driver will be loaded and all debugging messages will be
 available to see!
