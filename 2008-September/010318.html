 Ok, I see.

 Wouldn't it be a good idea to have mkindex
 a) check with the MIME database to verify the type of the attribute (maybe
 even do some automagic typing if the attribute exists in the MIME
 database)

 b) mention in the --help that 32bit is "int" and 64bit is "llong"? As long
 as there's only a commandline app to do the indexing, end users are
 exposed to it. Many (myself included :) ) don't know what llong means.

 preferences when you choose the type 8 or 16 bits, that this type cannot
 be indexed/queried.
