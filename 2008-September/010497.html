 intel_extreme driver with my 1920x1280 samsung display and the integrated
 i865 chip (with analog video).

 This started with r26762, and reverting back to r26761 fixes the problem.

 Choosing a fail-safe video resolution doesn't change anything, but
 choosing  "Use fail-safe video mode" from the safe mode options works
 (apparently in VESA mode).
