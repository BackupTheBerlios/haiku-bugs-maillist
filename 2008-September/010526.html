 This is on vmware, r27404.

 On BeOS you could switch back and forth between two workspaces with ALT+~.
 It doesn’t seem to work here, though that could be a keymap issue.

 In that case, would it be possible to have a shortcut that’s relative to
 the actual, physical keyboard layout, independent of the used keymap? It
 makes sense to have a quick CTRL+TAB switch between two running apps and
 e.g. an ALT+{{{^}}} (on my keyboard that’s the key to the left of the
 1-key) to switch between two workspaces. It’s a similar gesture and the
 keys are physically close to switching to workspaces directly (ALT+F1,
 ALT+F2...).
