 One thing about the recurring compat_control() calls that might help: "op
 10007" seems to be the ETHER_GET_LINK_STATE ioctl command (defined in
 headers/private/net/ether_driver.h) which is sent by update_link_state()
 in add-ons/kernel/network/devices/ethernet/ethernet.cpp.
 update_link_state() is in turned called by ethernet_up() in the same file.
