 {{{
 c++ -o foo.o -c "/boot/home/Sub Dir Spaces/test.cxx"
 and:
 g++ -o foo.o -c "/boot/home/Sub Dir Spaces/test.cxx"
 }}}
 where test.cxx is just a simple program.[[BR]]
 With the c++ line you get this error on Haiku:[[BR]]
 g++: cannot specify -o with -c or -S and multiple compilations
 but the g++ compiles it just fine.
