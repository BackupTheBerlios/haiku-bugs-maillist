 Somewhere between r29186 and r29227, the font rendering in Tracker-windows
 broke. It looks like the font rendering is done inverted (white text on
 black), but then put on screen with black text on a white background:
  - Subpixel anti-aliasing is inverted (blue colored edges on the left
 side, red on the right)
  - Text under a dragged icon has a black background

 Only the 'contents' of the Tracker-windows are affected, the menu for
 example isn't.

 I suspect changeset:29190 or changeset:29221 is the culprit.

 See attached screenshots for the differences in text-rendering between
 r29186 and r29227.
