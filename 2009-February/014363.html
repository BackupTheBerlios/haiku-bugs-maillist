 Mounting read-only a partition from a USB hard drive that have experienced
 at least bug #3400 (so probably not cleanly unmounted) result in this
 error after a few seconds :

 PANIC: tried to get writable block on a read-only cache !

 Once the partition is mounted, the bfs block allocator thread eat most of
 the CPU until the panic message.

 Tested in r29174.
