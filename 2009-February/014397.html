 Normally when resizing Terminal,  any existing and new lines of text will
 wrap as needed. However, there is one case where this fails: "AnyValue x
 9".  As soon as the number of rows is increased by resizing, text wrapping
 functions properly.

 As a note, I have not been able to resize the window while maintaining the
 same exact pixel height.

 The easiest way to reproduce this is to first resize Terminal to have a
 small X-axis value.  Then reduce the Y-axis to its smallest value.
 Finally, while keeping the Y-axis the same, increase the X-axis.
