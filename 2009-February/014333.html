 Since the coding guidelines specifically advise to use BObjectList over
 BList, ObjectList.h should be a public header, as application developers
 who try to stick to the coding guidelines as reference, even for
 applications not part of Haiku itself and thus not included in the
 repository are otherwise forced to build from within the Haiku build
 system or duplicate the header.
