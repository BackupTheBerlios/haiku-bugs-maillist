Comment(by anevilyak):

 The problem is that prior to FreeBSD7, the ethernet driver interrupt
 handlers had no way to return whether they handled interrupts or not. Our
 compat layer has not yet been updated to version 7, so as of right now all
 of our FreeBSD-based drivers cannot effectively do this. Updating the
 layer and drivers appropriately would probably fix this issue.
