 When you press the `stop` button while Installer is collecting the copy
 information, it won't stop until it's finished collecting.

 It should immediately stop, just like it does when copying files.

 Steps to reproduce:
  - Press the `Begin` button; Installer begins collecting copy information
  - Press the `Stop` button; a windows pops up asking if you're sure
  - Press that window's `Stop` button
  - You'll notice that Installer continues collecting copy information.
 Pressing the `Stop` button again has no effect and you'll have to wait for
 Installer to finish collecting before it gets back to the main screen.
