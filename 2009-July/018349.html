 This is r31444.

 I'm not a native speaker, but IMO the "Property" view/menu should actually
 be named "Properties". Just like with "Options" or "Settings". I
 considered providing a patch, but "Property" is used elsewhere besides
 that menu and I didn't want to mess things up... :)

 Because a circle path is added right away (no panel to enter the number of
 segments, for example) the ellipses at the end of "Add Circle..." can be
 removed (until that feature is implemented).

 IMO opinion "File | Append..." fits more to text files where you add more
 text to the end. I suggest to rename this function to "File | Insert..."
 or simply "File | Add...". Maybe it's just a matter of taste.
