 If you change 'Fixed font' setting to 'DejaVu Sans Mono Bold' and restart
 system, the system will crash during boot process.

 How to reproduce:[[BR]]
 1. go to Preferences->Fonts[[BR]]
 2. change 'Fixed font' to 'DejaVu Sans Mono Bold'[[BR]]
 3. restart the system[[BR]]
 4. system will crash during boot process.[[BR]]
 [[BR]]
 Tested in VirtualBox with r31876-gcc4.
