 The patch looks pretty nice to me, although I have not actually looked for
 any bugs. You have commit access, right? ;-) My suggestions for
 improvements would be two things:

 1) You should never use wording in the UI that makes the computer into a
 person ("Should I remove the file..."). Instead, use passive language like
 "Should the file be removed...". That sounds much more professional.

 2) Maybe "fCurrentPolicy" is not the best name. Policy for what? Perhaps
 "fCurrentItemExistsPolicy" would be better.
