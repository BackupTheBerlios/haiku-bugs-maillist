 When you get into the KDL you'll be greeted with the following
 screen:[[BR]]
 [[Image(KDL1.png)]][[BR]]
 Requesting help, gets you the first help-page:[[BR]]
 [[Image(KDL2.png)]][[BR]]
 You'll notice the two blank lines after the first row overwriting the
 `Welcome...` and `Thread...` rows. The `kdebug>` prompt is not overwritten
 however.

 The next page doesn't continue after the two blank lines, but right after
 the first line (notice that this page has not been fully written to the
 screen yet):[[BR]]
 [[Image(KDL3.png)]][[BR]]

 When the page has been fully written, the two blank lines now cover the
 first two lines of this new page as indicated by the red bounding
 rectangle:[[BR]]
 [[Image(KDL4.png)]][[BR]]

 From then on, every page has it's first two lines overwritten by the two
 blank lines.
