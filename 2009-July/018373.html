Comment(by stippi):

 It sounds very much like you are suffering from an interrupt storm. In the
 kernel debugger, you can use the "ints" command to get a listing. There
 should not be an interrupt line with thousands of unhandled interrupts.
 From there, you can also see which driver registered a handler. Best is
 probably to post the ints output here and maybe someone knows what could
 be done about it.
