 Fixed in r31912. Turns out I wasn't quite so senile... the previous
 mechanism was based on the idea that you could delete a *locked* BLocker,
 which would make a thread which blocked on the locked BLocker get
 B_BAD_SEM as result for it's locking request. The new mechanism is more
 straight forward and doesn't have a race condition that the previous code
 needed to take into account.
