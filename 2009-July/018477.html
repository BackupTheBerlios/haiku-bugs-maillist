 I tried to fix this by setting the feel of the TSwitcherWindow class to
 B_FLOATING_ALL_WINDOW_FEEL, but that did not fix the problem.

 To reproduce, simply move any app window toward the center of the screen,
 move over to the Deskbar, open it's menu, then Ctrl-Tab to the other app
 (leaving the Deskbar menu open.) Then press and hold Ctrl-Tab and you will
 see that the Twitcher window is hidden by the app window.
