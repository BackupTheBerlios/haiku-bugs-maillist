 Selection in the source code view has several issues:
  - Sometimes the selection doesn't begin until the mouse has been dragged
 several characters or even lines.
  - In some situations the selection is off one or more characters (i.e.
 when copying the selection, text at a different position is copied).
  - Sometimes lines cannot be selected up to the last character.
 Might be related to the handling of tabs in the code.
