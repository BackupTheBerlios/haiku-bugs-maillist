Comment (by ksmith):

 After further investigation, this seems to be the result of how undo works
 in the BTextView, which uses a BUndoBuffer. The BUndoBuffer never resets
 its state to B_UNDO_UNAVAILABLE when the undo buffer is empty, which is
 why the Undo menu item becomes permanently enabled.

 The Redo menu item is just enabled. There is no check for the availability
 of a Redo.
