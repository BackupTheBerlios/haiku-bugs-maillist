 Data CD auto mounted as an Audio CD

 Boot Haiku with a data cd or dvd inserted (tested iso, multitrack bfs) and
 it will get auto mounted as an audio cd on startup. Each track is showed
 as a .wav with the corresponding size.

 Tested in vmware and real hardware with r21760.
