 ok, I was able to re-create the bug.

 At the end of SaveAsDraft(), we are setting fSent to true. This never gets
 reset to false when you change text anywhere in the email. Since this
 remains true, we don't satisfy the conditions in the if statement of
 QuitRequested(). Setting fSent to false in the case Field_CHANGED of the
 MessageReceived() seems to fix this bug. However, I am not sure if this
 would introduce any other bugs.
