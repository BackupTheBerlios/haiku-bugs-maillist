#1340: Terminal doesn't produce the EOF character on Ctrl-D

 Platform:  All                      |

 It seems that the terminal produces a three character escape sequence when
 Ctrl-D is pressed instead of the correct (single) character. This causes
 Ctrl-D not to have the desired effect. E.g.
 {{{
 cat > /dev/null
 }}}
 can not be terminated with Ctrl-D. This works fine with Be's Terminal
 under Haiku.
