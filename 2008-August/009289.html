Comment(by andreasf):

 The attached patch changes {{{arch_setup_signal_frame}}} and
 {{{arch_restore_signal_frame}}} to store EBP in the {{{ebp}}} field and
 EBX in {{{_reserved_2[2]}}}, EBP's previous field.

 This was checked against real-world applications (make, sleep, vim, ftp)
 and helped with the ongoing port of Mono's JIT.
