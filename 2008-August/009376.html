 I'm not complaining about Mono issues here. Those I'm tracking over at:

 Vanilla Mono does run fine on OpenSolaris and other platforms. The only
 vaguely related change of mine that comes to mind is that Haiku did not
 have {{{madvise}}}, {{{mprotect}}} and the POSIX {{{sigaction}}} with

 (message / crash) in userland. If it's a bug in the kernel, I'd expect a
 KDL (to investigate), never just a freeze without info.
