 For some time, I am occasionally seeing Haiku freeze completely, i.e. it
 does not react to mouse movements or F12.

 Serial output says
 {{{
 CPU 0 halted
 }}}
 (or {{{CPU 1 halted}}}).

 There is however no indication what actually leads to this, i.e. the
 messages before differ, e.g., BFS warnings or "last message repeated two
 times" or runtime_loader unable to load {{{_APP_}}} or some non-existing
 file.

 In all instances this happened under load, running configure or make, last

 changes.)
