 with a different one. Adding an author list will not heal this error.

 year for example in wchar.t it's 2002.

 http://svn.berlios.de/viewcvs/haiku/haiku/trunk/headers/posix/wchar.h?rev=25433&view=log

 and 2008.

 - Copyright (C) 2002 Marcus Overhagen
 + Copyright 2002 Haiku Inc. All Rights Reserved.

 + Copyright 2002-2003, 2005-2008 Haiku Inc. All Rights Reserved.
