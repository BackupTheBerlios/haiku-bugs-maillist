 Certain fonts, specifically Calibri and Cambria of the Webcore fonts
 packages, are garbled when rendered at certain sizes, usually within 12 to
 17. This happens in the Fonts preflet, FontDemo app, and in StyledEdit.
 Probably happens elsewhere too.

 To reproduce, grab the [http://www.alkalay.net/software/webcore-fonts
 /webcore-fonts-3.0.tar.gz Webcore fonts] package, and install Calibri and
 Cambria (found in the Vista directory). In StyledEdit, type some text in
 one of those fonts at a size of 12.

 Tested with r21293 under VMware/Windows XP.
