#1258: Team Monitor may confuse people who Ctrl-Alt-Delete on a shut down system
----------------------------------+-----------------------------------------
 Reporter:  jonas.kirilla         |       Owner:  korli         
     Type:  enhancement           |      Status:  new           
 Priority:  normal                |   Milestone:  R1            
Component:  Servers/input_server  |     Version:  R1 development
 Platform:  All                   |  
----------------------------------+-----------------------------------------
 If you shut down, find yourself at the "System is Shut Down" window
 and press Ctrl-Alt-Delete, the Team Monitor opens and you're offered
 to have the Desktop restarted. That doesn't work from there.

 I believe the input_server knows that the system is shutting down,
 and if it is, or can be, told that the shutdown is complete, it might be
 a good thing to suppress the "Restart the Desktop" option, or perhaps
 even the entire Team Monitor.
