 Samba daamon (smbd) doesn't work because of broken getaddrinfo().
 The function return an error if a hint AI_GETADDRINFO is passed. I've made
 investigation and realized that AI_MASK in netdb.h is incorrect.

 Please look at:
 http://dev.haiku-
 os.org/browser/haiku/trunk/src/kits/network/dns/irs/getaddrinfo.c#L336
 and
 http://dev.haiku-os.org/browser/haiku/trunk/headers/posix/netdb.h#L206

 In my opinion AI_MASK should be defined as follow:

 {{{
 #define AI_MASK (AI_PASSIVE | AI_CANONNAME | AI_NUMERICHOST |
 AI_ADDRCONFIG)
 }}}
