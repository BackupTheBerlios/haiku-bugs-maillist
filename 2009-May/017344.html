 Wow, that was a long time ago, my memory doesn't go 3 weeks back so
 easily! If you want to obtain output from your replicant, you have to
 launch Tracker itself in a Terminal:

 in BeOS. I want to know the message format when archiving BScrollBar. If

 to drop the replicant onto your own TextView and thereby print the archive
 contents to the Terminal.
