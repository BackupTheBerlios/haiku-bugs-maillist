 This [http://www.freelists.org/post/haiku-development/OptionalPackages-
 how-to-properly-package-its-dependencies,1 haiku-development mailing list
 thread] raised the issue of having .OptionalPackageDescriptions for the
 same piece of software. Such a situation may occur when giving attribution
 to an OptionalPackage's included dependencies. A specific example is
 libxml2. Currently it is listed in AboutSystem. Once NetSurf is repackaged
 to include a more complete .OptionalPackageDescription, it will also give
 recognition to libxml2.
