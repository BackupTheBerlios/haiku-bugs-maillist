Comment(by tigerdog):

 This sounds similar to the trouble I have here with a 5600 card.  Haiku is
 unable to distinguish my monitor's max supported resolution and tries to
 start in a mode higher than the monitor supports.  I work around it by
 hitting space during boot, manually setting fail-safe video mode to
 120x1024 then choosing safe mode options and use fail-safe video.

 Once the system starts, I choose a _different_ supported resolution using
 Screen Preferences.  After this, a restart brings the system up normally
 and I can set my desired mode which stays permanently.
