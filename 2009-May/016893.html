Comment(by stippi):

 You are probably mixing up different things. DrawAfterChildren() is only
 called if your (parent) view has the flag "B_DRAW_ON_CHILDREN" set.
 BTabViews do not have this flag set, unless you manually added this flag
 yourself. If you have not done so, this bug is invalid. Have you added the
 flag?
