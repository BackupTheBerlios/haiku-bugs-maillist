 Haiku does not allows mounting two filesystems on the same directory : the
 second mount fails with "Device/File/Resource busy". But after this,
 unmounting the filesystem also fails with the same error.
 {{{
 > mount -t fat mydisk1.img /fat
 > unmount /fat
 > mount -t fat mydisk1.img /fat
 > mount -t fat mydisk2.img /fat
 mount: Device/File/Resource busy
 > unmount /fat
 unmount: unmounting failed: Device/File/Resource busy
 }}}
