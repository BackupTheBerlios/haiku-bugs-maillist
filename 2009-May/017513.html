 I attached a patch for the unix protocol add-on. It makes
 UnixEndpoint::Receivable() return ENOTCONN once all data has been read, if
 the other end shut down the connection. The testcase passes with this
 change.

 The tcp protocol add-on seems to be working the same way, yet I am not
 certain this is the right way to fix it - comments are welcome.
