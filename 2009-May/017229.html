 According to Open Group, {{{struct stat}}} has mandatory members
 {{{st_mtim}}} etc.
 http://www.opengroup.org/onlinepubs/9699919799/basedefs/sys_stat.h.html

 In [browser:haiku/trunk/headers/posix/sys/stat.h posix/sys/stat.h] they
 are called {{{st_mtime}}} etc. instead. Notice the extra letter.

 This breaks recent Git builds.

 Is this POSIX deviation for BeOS compatibility, or did simply no one
 complain yet?
