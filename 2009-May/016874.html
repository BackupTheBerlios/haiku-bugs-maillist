 If you have StyledEdit and/or Mail open with a changed document and then
 select shutdown from the deskbar menu, the respective alerts asking
 whether or not to save the document appear, but only for a couple of
 seconds, then the system proceeds to shut down.

 Expected behaviour would be to wait until the user answers the dialog
 (possibly showing an additional hint that the application may be waiting
 for that answer).

 I've seen this in VMware with both gcc2 and gcc4 builds.
