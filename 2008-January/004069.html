 VLC crashed when trying to close a streaming asx video from NASA TV. The
 steps to reproduce are:
 1) Open an asx file that connects to a internet video stream.
 2) After it starts playing click the VLC stop button.
 3) Click the close button in the window tab.
 VLC does not close. The way I closed it was by killing the w>Messages
 thread from process controller. It was using most of the CPU cycles.
 The expected behaviour is that VLC should close its window and all
 associated threads.
 I was using VLC 0.86C and haiku.image.23198
