 When opening a sub-menu, it should open in the same direction as its
 parent did.

 For example, if the Deskbar is in the top right corner, the first level
 menus all open to the left, as there is no space on the usual right side.
 However, the sub-menus sometimes open on the left, sometimes on the right,
 depending on their width. This is very confusing; the menus shouldn't
 change their opening direction.
