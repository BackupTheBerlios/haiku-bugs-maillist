 Since the mbox2mail and mail2mbox command line utils were recently
 modified, their Usage info has become incorrect. Specifically...

 {{{
 bemailtombox InputDirectory >OutputFile
 }}}

 ...should be:

 {{{
 mail2mbox InputDirectory >OutputFile
 }}}

 And...

 {{{
 mboxtobemail [-s] InputFile [OutputDirectory]
 }}}

 ...should be:

 {{{
 mbox2mail [-s] InputFile [OutputDirectory]
 }}}
