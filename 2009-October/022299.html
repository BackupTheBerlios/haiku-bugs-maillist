 The horizontal scrollbar ignores a range given to it by
 BTextView::SetTextRect(BRect) when the width of the TextRect is larger
 than the visible rectangle and word wrap is turned off.

 To reproduce, open StyledEdit, turn off word wrap and type a long sentence
 that goes beyond the visible frame. The horizontal scrollbar does not
 update as expected and any non-visible text cannot be reached.

 (I reproduced this bug in r32959 but I have heard that it still occurs in
 later revisions.)
