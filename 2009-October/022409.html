 When you have a couple of images in a folder, named:
 {{{
 1a
 12
 b
 c
 }}}
 You'll notice that when you cycle through them with ShowImage, it will
 stop at the last image (`c`), but it alternates between the first (`1a`)
 and second (`12`).

 When you rename the first image to `1`, ShowImage will also stop at the
 first image.

 I think this bug is caused by tracker's [changeset:29845 natural sorting
 algorithm], where one program (ShowImage) thinks that `12` comes before
 `1a` and the other (Tracker) thinks `12` comes after `1a`.
