 This is r33529.

 Mail's "Mail" symbol in the toolbar seems to open a hard coded ~/mail.
 I've moved my mail folder to another partition. I also have subfolders for
 my different mail accounts.

 The Mail symbol should open the mail folder of the currently used account
 ("From" drop-down menu) as defined in the E-mail preferences. Since the in
 and out box can be in totally different locations, I'd say go for the
 inbox's parent folder...[[BR]]
 Or change the E-mail preferences to only take one location per account and
 create the in, out, drafts folders as subfolders there. I'd prefer that,
 actually...
