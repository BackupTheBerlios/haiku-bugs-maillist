Comment(by mmlr):

 Write performance should be considerably better since r33507. Due to how
 the caches allocate pages it is possible though that due to physical page
 fragmentation performance degrades right down to the worst case scenario
 it was before. This should only be seen when saturating physical memory,
 i.e. when transferring large files so that the caches are exhausted. I'm
 investigating different allocation strategies and other possible
 optimizations to further optimize this though.
