Comment(by jackburton):

 The problem is that the GL addon doesn't redraw to the screen if the
 teapot is not spinning: since nothing changed from the openGL POV, it
 doesn't trigger a redraw. To fix this, we could to copy the content of the
 openGL "front" buffer to the screen on every DirectConnected() (possible,
 but right now I don't know if there are drawbacks).
