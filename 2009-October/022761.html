 Unticking the "Horizontal Scrolling" option in the Trackpad Preferences
 window does not actually prevent horizontal scrolling. The only way to do
 that is to move the red line in the scrolling frame of the window down to
 the bottom.

 To test, untick it and you can scroll Back in BeZillaBrowser by moving
 horizontally left.
