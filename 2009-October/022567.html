Comment(by idefix):

 According to FileTypes, `text/x-patch` can have one of the following
 extensions:
  - `.diff`
  - `.patch`
 And it's recognition rule is:
 {{{
 0.30          ([0:100]"diff " | [0:100]"--- " | [0:100]"+++ " | [0:100]"@@
 -")
 }}}
