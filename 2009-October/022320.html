Comment:

 The problem as I said is interrupts are disabled. This applies to all
 CPUs, not just the current one, as they must all enter KDL when a panic
 happens. However, since they're disabled, you cannot use any idling
 techniques, since those invariably involve sleeping until an interrupt.
 Trying to do that with ints disabled would permanently kill the session
 since you'd never be able to wake the CPU up again without completely
 resetting the system. As such this is simply an unavoidable consequence.
