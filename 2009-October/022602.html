Comment(by humdinger):

 Next time Tracker crashes, click the "Debug" button of the alert
 announcing the crash. This opens the debugger where you enter "bt" (for
 "backtrace") which returns useful info how it happened that you should
 include in your ticket.

 You can restart a crashed Tracker (or Deskbar) by invoking the TeamMonitor
 with CTRL+ALT+DEL and its "Restart Desktop" button. If Tracker/Deskbar
 froze instead of a nice fullblown crash, you'll have to kill its entry in
 the TeamMonitor list first.
