 After having mounted and unmounted a BFS image file the space occupied by
 the file is not freed when the file is deleted.

 To reproduce:
 Take note of the amount of free space on your Haiku volume.[[BR]]
 Expand i.e. this file: http://haiku-files.org/files/blank-bfs/blank-bfs-
 512mb.image.zip [[BR]]
 then mount it (doubleclick in Tracker), unmount it (through Tracker)[[BR]]
 Delete the image file[[BR]]
 Observe free space on volume, the 512 MB is not freed, not even after
 reboot.

 System used:
 R1/alpha (Revision 32945)[[BR]]
 On real hardware: 512 MB of memory, 640 MB swap, partition ~2.5 GB

 Tested the same file in BeOS R5, space was freed up afterwards.
