 You can move through the buttons of an alert with tab/shift+tab. I'd find
 it useful to have the cursor keys work, too. And don't wrap the button-
 switching around, i.e. once you reached the left-most button, the left-
 cursor-key won't jump to the right-most button, but stays on the left-
 most.[[BR]]
 That way if I know I want the left-most button, I hit cursor-left a few
 times and engage with return/space.

 Plus, have ESC always cancel.

 See also #2615 for using triggers.
