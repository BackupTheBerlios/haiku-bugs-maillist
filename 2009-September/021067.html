Comment(by mmadia):

 To note, building Haiku on filesystems that rely upon emulating attributes
 is not recommended.  Recommended filesystems include: BFS, UFS2, ReiserFS.
 See http://www.haiku-os.org/guides/building/configure/use-xattr for more
 info.
