 This is r32931.

 Taking a screenshot of a modal window from an app doesn't quite work as
 expected. Try this:

  * Open DriveSetup.
  * Choose a partition and invoke "Initialize". Continue to the dialog for
 setting name and blocksize. (Don't press the "Initialize" button! :) )
  * Open Screenshot, set options to capture active window and include
 border, delay 2 seconds.
  * Take screenshot and click on the modal window of DriveSetup.

 -> The Screenshot app doesn't come up again after the delay. Only after
 you close the modal window you'll get your screenshot, though without the
 border you explicitely asked to include.
