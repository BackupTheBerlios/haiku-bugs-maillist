 r33297, gcc2hybrid[[BR]]
 With these steps, an application will not be brought to front, upon
 regaining focus.[[BR]]

 To reproduce[[BR]]
 Open workspaces.[[BR]]
 start a few programs on two or more workspaces.[[BR]]
 eg, StyledEdit on workspace 1,  Terminal on workspace 2.[[BR]]
 Give StlyedEdit focus.[[BR]]
 Using Workspaces's window, drag Terminal form W2 to W1, make sure to not
 drag over StlyedEdit's Title tab, but cover up some of StyledEdit[[BR]]
 Click inside StyledEdit. [[BR]]
 It will gain focus and remain behind Terminal.[[BR]]
 When dragging, if the Title tab is dragged over, normal behavior is
 observed.[[BR]]
