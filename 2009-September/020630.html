 * Open Media preferences,
  * Enable Audio Settings > Show Volume Control on Deskbar,
  * Switch back to Audio Mixer, Gain Controls tab.
  * In Deskbar notify area, left click on Volume Control icon,
  * Drag it via replicant little hand on the desktop.
  * Drag Volume Control slider to full, half, etc

 => In Media Preferences, Audio Mixer, Gain Controls tab, the Master Output
 slider don't live update to show the new mixer master gain level.

  * Switch to Media window and drag the Master Output vertical slider, up
 and dow
  * Volume Control replicant on desktop live update!
  * Switch to Volume Control again and drag its slider
  * This time, Media Master Output update in live too !
  * Check Mute On

 => Volume Control still show volume as non-muted.

 Look like some media parameter is not always sync'ed  as it should be, and
 volume control don't listen to Master Output Mute parameter.
