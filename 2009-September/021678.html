 How to reproduce:

 - Right-click on a volume on the Tracker desktop and choose "Get Info".

 Experienced behavior:

 - The "Capacity:" line is displayed truncated, e.g. "Capacity: 599.5 MB
 (534.1 M...d -- 65.4 MB free)" because the info window is too small and
 cannot be resized.

 visible.
