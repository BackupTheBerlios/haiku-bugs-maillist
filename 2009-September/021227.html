Comment:

 Unfortunately, your native resolution (1920x1080) is not in the VESA BIOS
 mode list. There is 1920x1440 in the list, but I am not sure if that
 resolution is handled in our common accelerant code. It wouldn't help you
 in any case. So I would suggest to close this bug as invalid, since the
 VESA driver can indeed not offer your native resolution, and is thus
 behaving correctly. Please reopen if I missed something.
