 I have an old cd-burner that won't burn cd's when DMA is turned on for
 this device.

 Under BeOS I could selectively disable/enable DMA for specific devices
 with the `ata` and `atapi` files located in
 `~/config/settings/kernel/drivers` (see [http://betips.net/1997/09/09
 /disable-ide-dma-permanently/ this tip]).

 It would be nice if I could do the same in Haiku, so that DMA would be
 disabled for the cd-burner but enabled for the harddisks.
