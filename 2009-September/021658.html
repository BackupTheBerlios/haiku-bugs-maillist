 This is r33154.

 When you click the mail_daemon in the Deskbar, ~/mail opens. I knew that
 you could change what is opened, but couldn't remember how it's done.
 Scot's BeOS Bible came to the rescue:

 A link called "mailbox" in ~/mail points to the app/document/folder/query
 that will open on a click.

 Haiku is missing this "mailbox" link. Put it in there and it works as
 expected.

 Only, it doesn't really fit into ~/mail/.[[BR]]
 It'd much better fit into ~/config/settings/Mail/ where there's already
 the "Menu Links" with the stuff appearing in mail_daemon's context menu.

 Maybe it should also be set in the E-mail preferences, where there's
 already a link to open the ~/config/settings/Mail/Menu Links for
 customizing.
