Comment(by mmlr):

 That's interesting. This call doesn't actually do much. It just sets the
 static variable to the CPU count. Maybe the CPU count is messed up? You
 could do something like:

 {{{
 for (uint32 i = 0; i < bootKernelArgs->num_cpus; i++) spin(10 * 1000 *
 1000);
 }}}

 This would add a 10 second delay for every CPU. Then you could simply time
 the time it takes to reboot and deduce the count from that. Can you also
 post a picture of the early debug output you get when pressing escape? The
 numbers should also be there.
