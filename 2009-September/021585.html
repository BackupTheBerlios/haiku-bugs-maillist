Comment(by mmlr):

 It's most likely that you don't get (all) timer interrupts, therefore the
 reschedule events won't happen. So the idle thread, once it is running,
 won't ever get interrupted. You can verify that by checking the ints
 command and note down the numbers, continue from there and recheck after a
 bit of time has passed. If the timer interrupts aren't counted up then
 that's your problem. From the rest of what you report it really looks that
 way in any case.
