 When you (force) unmount the volume from which MediaPlayer is playing a
 movie, its high priority (real time) threads will suck up all of the CPU
 power (the mouse cursor still works, though).

 While the kernel might want to unreal those threads automatically (at
 least temporarily), I'd guess MediaPlayer could handle this case better.
