 I found two reproductible bugs related to IDE DMA under VMWare.

 1) When trying to shutdown Haiku, the shutdown is stalling for several
 seconds on  "Tidying things up a bit" showing no icon or indication of
 progress.

 2) wget and ftp both stall randomly when downloading a file.

 Setting "Disable IDE DMA" in the boot menu solves the two problems.

 I'm attaching the serial output of a simple startup and shutdown session
 with and without IDE DMA enabled.
