 The MSI NX7600GT (nvidia GeForce 7600GT) has vendor id 0x10de and device
 id 0x02e0, which is not in the device list, so I manually added it to the
 driver.c

 Zeta 1.2 will reject the driver ("Your card is currently not supported")
 and boot in grey scale or VESA.

 Haiku crashes (SEGFAULT in app_server) and leaves the system unuasable at
 the last icon of the boot screen. (KDL over serial works).
 Using "fail-safe video mode" Haiku boots and the Vesa-mode works fine (a
 bit slow though).

 Tested with R26149 and R26151, serial output is almost identical each
 time.

 May be related to #1518 and #2071.
