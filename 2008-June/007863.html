 It seems like some motherboards with ATI chipsets don't route IRQs
 normally through the PIT.  My HP laptop stalls unless I press keys or move
 the mouse to generate interrupts.

 Laptop is HP6715b (ATI Xpress 1250)
 My Desktop with Asus A8R-MVP mobo is also affected. (ATI CrossFire™ Xpress
 1600 chipset)

 Linux prints the following clues:

 ATI board detected. Disabling timer routing over 8254.
