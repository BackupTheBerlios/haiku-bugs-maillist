Comment:

 Should be fixed in r26128. The intel partitioning system would be
 associated with the disk as it just indicated supporting it with a
 priority of 0.1 and the actual FAT filesystem module that would have
 supported it with a higher priority wasn't loaded that early. Now the
 intel partitioning system will just leave it unsupported when it finds
 itself in a unlikely useful situation, therefore giving the second scan
 round with the FAT module available a chance of correctly identifying the
 partition/filesystem.
