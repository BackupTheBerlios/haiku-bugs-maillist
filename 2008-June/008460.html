 1. Switch to icon mode and move one or more icons on the boundaries so
 that it augments the icons extent and activates the scrollbars.

 2. Now scroll or adjust to your liking.

 3. Switch to mini-icon mode and back to icon mode.

 -> The icons are now offseted like in 1).

 The previous and expected behavior is to show the icon as they were in 2).

 The bug appeared the first time with r26043, disapeared with r26047, and
 re-appeared with r26057.

 Apparently some scrolling that was previously blocked by the bounds check
 in BView::ScrollTo/By has been "reactivated".
