 Making the partition empty by removing all files does _not_ necessarily
 cure a corrupted filesystem. If you want to start over with a filesystem,
 you should just use DriveSetup to reinitialize said partition with a
 completely new BFS. That should resolve this kind of problem. Closing the
 ticket as it is a BeOS/BFS issue.
