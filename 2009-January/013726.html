 Some existing compiled resource files that where generated on Zeta are not
 correctly handled by Haiku. On the attached file, Haiku detects the
 app_flags as 0x4000000 instead of 0x4 (B_BACKGROUND_APP).
 I do not remember on what zeta version was generated the attached file,
 but it begins with "Joy!resf" instead of "RS".
 New files generated on Zeta 1.21 are correctly recognized by Haiku. This
 may be something specific to an old Zeta release. I do not have a BeOS
 install here to test.
