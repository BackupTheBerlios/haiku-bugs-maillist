Comment(by anevilyak):

 More information: MediaPlayer appears to be rejecting them based off
 mimetype. The sniffer rules identify them as application/ogg by default,
 which is correct. Forcing the type to audio/vorbis makes MediaPlayer play
 them correctly. Note that according to http://www.iana.org/assignments
 /media-types/ , application/ogg, audio/ogg, audio/vorbis and video/ogg are
 all allowed/correct.
