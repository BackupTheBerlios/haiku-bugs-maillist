Comment(by luroh):

 Repeatable on real hw as well:[[BR]][[BR]]
 1. dd a haiku-image to a USB stick and a hard disk.[[BR]]
 2. Boot from the USB stick.[[BR]]
 3. Mount and remove all files from the hard disk.[[BR]]
 4. Try to boot from the USB stick again.[[BR]]
 5. No profit.[[BR]][[BR]]
 In other words, you can render your system disk unbootable by removing
 files from another disk. Sounds potentially dangerous to me.
