 USB to LAN, USB to COM, USB to LPT, USB to PS/2 and USB to Audio. All
 those devices normally detected under Windows (see screenshoot in
 attachment). But by connect this device under Haiku normally detected only

 all devices detected on those ports. For example: USB Flash and USB HDD

 other USB hub and find identical problem: USB mouse not work ''after'' USB
 hub.
