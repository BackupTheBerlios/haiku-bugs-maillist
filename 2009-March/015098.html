 The bottom window status line could be extended to show the space the
 objects in the active window use on the disk next to their number. Make
 this a user setting via Tracker prefs. Of course symlinks would be
 excluded, as they are not part of the active window. Actually, as their
 object size is zero, it probably does not even matter.

 Extended statusline example:
 14 items | 3,06 Mb
