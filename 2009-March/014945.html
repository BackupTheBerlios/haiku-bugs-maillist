 Revision 29436, Vmware Player 2.5.0 (Ubuntu 8.10, 32-bit).[[BR]]
 [[BR]]
 1. Build a vanilla 300 MB haiku-vmware-image.[[BR]]
 2. Make a copy of it named haiku_copy.vmdk[[BR]]
 3. Include the image copy in the haiku.vmx file:[[BR]]
    ide0:1.present = "TRUE"[[BR]]
    ide0:1.fileName = "haiku_copy.vmdk"[[BR]]
    ide0:1.deviceType = "disk"[[BR]]
    ide0:1.mode = "persistent"[[BR]]
    ide0:1.redo = ""[[BR]]
    ide0:1.writeThrough = "FALSE"[[BR]]
    ide0:1.startConnected = "FALSE"[[BR]]
 4. Boot.[[BR]]
 5. From the Haiku desktop, mount the copy image.[[BR]]
 6. Double-click the copy image disk, press <Alt + A> and <Shift + Delete>
 to remove all files.[[BR]]
