 /boot/home/config/be/Preferences is a symlink to /boot/beos/preferences.
 This is incorrect.

 It should be an actual folder like /boot/home/config/be/Applications.
 This will allow 3rd party preflets, such as IM Kit and InfoPopper to be
 installed to /boot/preferences and symlinked to
 /boot/home/config/be/Preferences and displayed in Deskbar.
