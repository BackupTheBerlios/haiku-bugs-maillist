 The button assignment menu pops up when:

 * the Mouse preferences window is open, and

 * a window is in front of it, and

 * the pointer is in the area where the mouse buttons were in the image on
 the Mouse preferences window

 * a mouse button is pressed

 The same happens if the Mouse preferences window is minimized. Clicking on
 the desktop or a window in the right area will pop up the menu.
