#3540: Twitcher leaks pressed Tab-key to previously activated window

 Priority:  normal                |   Milestone:  R1

 When you open Twitcher by holding Ctrl-Tab for a second, the previously
 activated window gets the Tab-key signals until you release the Tab-
 key.[[BR]]
 Tested with Haiku r29463.

 Steps to reproduce:
  - Open a Terminal window or a Tracker window
  - When that window is activated press and hold the Ctrl-key and then
 press and hold the Tab-key
  - After a second, the Twitcher window opens
  - Immediately after opening, the previously activated window react to a
 continuously pressed Tab-key:
   - A Terminal window displays: "Display all 501 possibilities? (y or n)"
   - A Tracker window displays a moving file selection

  - Release the Tab-key (but not the Ctrl-key)
  - Press the Tab-key again:
   - The application-selection in Twitcher changes, but the previously
 activated window won't react to the Tab-key anymore

 Preferred behaviour:
  - The previously activated window shouldn't react to the pressed Tab-key
