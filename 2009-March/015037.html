 Attached you'll find an OpenSearch ticket search plugin for firefox
 (supports IE7 too).[[BR]]
 This one is specifically targeted at ticket search as it prepends the '#'
 to your query. Just enter your ticket number and voila :)[[BR]]
 You can test it by copying it to your firefox profile/searchplugins
 directory (note it will alter it by replacing the icon url with a cached
 version)[[BR]]

 If accepted, can a web maintainer add support for the plugin's auto
 discovery to the site. It should be one line added to the <head> section.
 See
 https://developer.mozilla.org/en/Creating_OpenSearch_plugins_for_Firefox
