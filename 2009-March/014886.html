 This is r29397.

 With the drop-down menu to the top left you can choose the volume to
 examine. You can also switch between volumes while one is being scanned.

 If tabs were used instead, we could do away with the whole bar at the top.
 Just one tab per mounted volume and a button "Scan volume" in the middle
 of every tab's view if it's not already scanned.

 If volumes or folders are drag&dropped into DiskUsage, the tab of the
 according volume would pop to the front and the location would change to
 the dropped folder and the scanning starts.

 With node-monitoring newly mounted volumes can be added automatically as
 tabs.
 Also node-monitoring would remove the need to rescan. It's the Haiku way.
