Comment(by anevilyak):

 That depends on how the camera exposes itself. Some cameras expose
 themselves as USB mass storage, and as such their on board SD cards wind
 up looking like an ordinary hard disk. PTP cameras do not do this however,
 so making them appear in Tracker would be significantly more involved, and
 would probably involve writing a pseudo-filesystem to make the PTP camera
 appear like a disk. This would probably be doable via libptp and
 userlandfs, but it's certainly not trivial.
