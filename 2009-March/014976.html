 Running Haiku r29436 on a Core 2 Quad 6600 with 4 cores:

 1. Stress the system with 4 Terminals running "cat /dev/zero > /dev/null"

 2. Observe Pulse indicate near 100% CPU use on all four cores.

 3. Open the Applications folder, Alt-A, Alt-O and wait for them to load

 4. Observe in Pulse how scheduling stops on some cores. If you pause
 scheduling on the remaining/working cores Haiku freezes.

 This has also been seen in VMware by Phadin on #Haiku.
