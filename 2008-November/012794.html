Comment(by Adek336):

 I found the proper solution.

 The file ~/.bashrc must contain the line
 {{{
 shopt -s checkwinsize
 }}}

 Also under a linux install, you will notice that removing this line from
 ~/.bashrc enables one to reproduce the bug under linux.
