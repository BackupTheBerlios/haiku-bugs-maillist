 The {{{V*}}} macros are just indices into termios::c_cc. I doubt that the
 termios structure or the indices are ever sent over the ssh connection. Or
 if they are, I'm pretty sure the OpenSSH authors have considered that
 those indices can differ (they are also different in Linux). I wouldn't
 expect the problem there.

 It is more likely that certain xterm features are used that Haiku's
 Terminal doesn't support yet. Or, of course, there might still be bugs in
 the supported features.
