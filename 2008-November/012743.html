 but this bug is specific to haikuware.com home page.

 1. go to: www.haikuware.com in Firefox.
 2. Click on any link that will stay in haikuware but leave the home page.
 3. Click on the home page link.

 browse from that window to open with Firefox, it will open the download
 window for downloading "index.php".
