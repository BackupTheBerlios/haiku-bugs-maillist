Comment(by umccullough):

 I personally don't find the visual designation of boot stages useful
 unless there's a specific stage that might hang or fail, where the visual
 indication could be used to identify the next step of troubleshooting.

 Thus, if an icon represents a boot stage that is highly unlikely to fail,
 or vary in length, it's pretty much useless to me.
