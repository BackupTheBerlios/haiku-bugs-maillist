Comment(by axeld):

 Not sure if OS.h is the right place for that (as it's a system specific
 extension of the pthread API), but having a pthread object (which could
 even be named differently, then) for each thread sounds like a viable
 solution.

 That won't simplify convert_to_pthread(), however. It would be possible to
 have a syscall that returns the TLS space of any thread, but due to race
 conditions, it might be better to have a syscall that reads only specific
 fields inside that TLS space.
