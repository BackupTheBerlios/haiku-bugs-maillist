 I sometimes see special rectangle characters in some mail's subject and
 thread attributes. I have no idea what they are (tabs?) and where they
 come from, but they mess up ordering new mail by "Thread" and "When".
 Unfortunately, screenshooting KDLs Haiku ATM...

 As examples, I have http://www.freelists.org/post/haiku-development/Im-
 interested-in-developing-a-project-for-Haiku,27
 and http://www.freelists.org/post/haiku-development/Im-interested-in-
 developing-a-project-for-Haiku,25
 which appear OK in the archive, but with/without that special character
 when fetched by the mail_daemon.

 Could the mail_daemon intelligently filter out those characters when
 parsing mails?
