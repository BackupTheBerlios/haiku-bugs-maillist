Comment(by leavengood):

 I don't know if you want to go this far (and this is definitely in R2
 territory) but another nice feature from Win7 and Mac OS X is the
 customizable context menu available on right click of an app in the
 taskbar or Dock. Basically the app can export a menu that gets shown on
 right click, in addition to the standard options (such as those you are
 proposing.)

 This would not be difficult but it would require some sort of protocol
 between the Deskbar and the apps to build the menu. Then there are the
 details of whether the Deskbar queries the app for the menu each time, or
 if the app exports it and then updates it as needed whenever something
 changes. But those are easy enough to work out.
