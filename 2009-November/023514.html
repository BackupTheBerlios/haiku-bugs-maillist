 This is r33893.

 With ALT+1,2,3... there's a quick way to append a predefined signature to
 your Mail. If you don't find the right one on first try, however, pressing
 ALT+1,2,3... will add one sig under the other.

 It'd be much nicer to always have a signature replace the one inserted
 before. In order not having to track what's a signature, this could be as
 easy as to keep an inserted sig text selected. That way inserting another
 one will simply replace it.
