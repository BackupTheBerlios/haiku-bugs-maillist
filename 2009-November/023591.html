 POSIX.1-2008 introduces standard functions to operate on FD + relative
 path pairs:
  - faccessat()
  - fchmodat()
  - fchownat()
  - fstatat()
  - linkat()
  - mkdirat()
  - mkfifoat()
  - mknodat()
  - openat()
  - readlinkat()
  - renameat()
  - symlinkat()
  - unlinkat()
  - utimensat()

 The new open modes O_SEARCH AND O_EXEC should also be implemented.
