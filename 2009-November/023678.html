Comment(by ajitomatix):

 I have found a workaround thanks to [http://betips.net/1997/09/09/intel-
 ca810-video-with-other-graphics-adapters/ this BeOS R5 tip].

 Since intel extreme driver is not working, the workaround is to use a VESA
 driver. To do that I did the following:

 {{{
 $> cd /boot/system/add-ons/kernel/drivers/dev/graphics/
 $> sudo mv intel_extreme ~/
 }}}
 Then restart Haiku.

 What this does is removes the symbolic link to the buggy intel_extreme
 driver. Haiku then has to fall back to VESA driver when it reboots.

 This workaround is tested on R33964.
