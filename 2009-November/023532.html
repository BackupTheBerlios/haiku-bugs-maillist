 This is r33939.

 Choosing "Favorites | Configure Favorites..." from an Open/Save panel
 opens a special panel similar what was available for configuring the
 Deskbar's ~/config/be/ menu in the past. As with the Deskbar, the favorite
 menu should be rename "Favorites | Edit Favorites..." and simply open
 ~/config/settings/Tracker/Go/.

 Also, once Favorites are available, the heading in the Open/Save panel
 Favorites-menu shouldn't be "Favorite Folders", but simply "Favorites",
 because those can also be mere files.
