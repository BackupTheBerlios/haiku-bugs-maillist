 When you enter an escape sequence such as '\n' into the StyledEdit find
 dialog, close and then reopen the find dialog, the text has become garbled
 (Extended ASCII characters or something similar, it's too small for me to
 make out).

 This does not occur with non-special escape sequences like \k, \p but with
 many 'special' ones like \n, \x. It occurs regardless of whether one
 clicks search or close.

 Tested under VMWarePlayer, Gentoo Linux, revision 20387.
