 Currently we don't have a replacement for BDB, the Be DeBugger. We use gdb
 on the command line, but it requires knowing the commands.
 Most likely a native GUI front-end to gdb would do the job.
 Gdb already has several front-ends for X11 based OSes, which could serve
 as examples on how to do it.
