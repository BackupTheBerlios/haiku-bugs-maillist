 The "home link", i.e. the link of the Haiku image at the top of a page,
 doesn't point to the actual home page for pages in subdomains.
 E.g.:

 {{{
 http://dev.haiku-os.org/newticket -> http://dev.haiku-os.org/
 http://factory.haiku-os.org/ -> http://factory.haiku-os.org/
 }}}

 I suppose, this is not intended.
