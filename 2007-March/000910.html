Comment:

 This bug report is spot on (except its title) and should be re-opened.

 My PS/2 keyboard won't work unless there's also a PS/2 mouse.

 To have a working keyboard in Haiku I have to either plug in a PS/2 mouse
 (to kickstart the PS/2 keyboard, somehow), or use a separate USB keyboard
 instead.

 I suppose using a USB mouse in PS/2 legacy mode, as described, tickles the
 PS/2 bus manager or whatever, in a similar way as plugging a real PS/2
 mouse, resulting in a working PS/2 keyboard.

 The PS/2 keyboard always works in the KDL, however. (When the rest is
 dead, at least the keyboard works.. The irony!)

 BTW, you can't enter the KDL voluntarily (with F12) from a USB keyboard,
 IIRC.
