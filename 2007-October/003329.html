Comment (by humdinger):

 I'd argue for the second solution, i.e. fallback to the preferred app for
 that MIME type as set in the FileTypes preferences. That's what it is for,
 you determine which app you want to open this kind of file by default. So,
 when you don't have the app installed that your collegue, who mailed you
 the file, prefers for that particular file, your own favorite app is
 launched.

 Just imagine your annoying collegue always sets BEOS:PREF_APP to "emacs"
 and you have only the correct editor installed: "vi"... :)
