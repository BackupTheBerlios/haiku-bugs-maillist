Comment (by umccullough):

 Nice call Axel!

 I added the following to my intel_gart.cpp:

 {0x2560, 0x2562, INTEL_TYPE_83x, "i845G"},

 And this clearly changed the driver behavior as it booted into a default
 to 800x600 (on a laptop display of 1024x768, this made it tile the
 screen)...

 I was able to then use the Screen prefs to change the resolution to
 1024x768x32bpp on the fly without reboot.

 I also got a slightly faster GLTeapot FPS, something like 110-120fps :)

 Sounds like a good change to commit.
