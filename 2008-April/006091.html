 I have a Dell Latitude D600 that has AC97 audio and a plain old cd drive.
 When I put an audio CD in and load CD Player I am able to play the CD as
 well as move back and forth between tracks.

 The problem happens when I press the stop button. The CD Player pauses the
 song for about .5 seconds and then resumes playing. Even when I exit the
 CD Player it continues to play.

 If I click the eject button and the cd is ejected it stops playing. That
 is the only way I have been able to find to stop the playback other than a
 reboot or killing media_server.

 This error happened in Rev 24761 and 24775
