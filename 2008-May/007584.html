#2258: resize-button in tab changes color sometimes
----------------------------+-----------------------------------------------
 Reporter:  jdawin          |       Owner:  stippi        
     Type:  bug             |      Status:  new           
 Priority:  normal          |   Milestone:  R1            
Component:  User Interface  |     Version:  R1 development
 Platform:  x86             |  
----------------------------+-----------------------------------------------
 Under certain conditions, the resize- and close-buttons in the tab change
 their color
 on clicking. To reproduze this behaviour do this:

 1. open a folder, say "Haiku" from the Desktop
 2. open another folder inside the first one, say "beos"
 3. open yet another inside this second folder, say "etc"
 4. now activate the "Haiku"-window, and maximize it, if it isn't already
 5. make it smaller by dragging the bottom-right edge
 6. scroll one the scrollbars
 7. move the window around
 8. click on the resize-button. Now, or after a few more clicks on the
 resize-button
 the button should change its color to dark blue or grey, which is
 unexpected.
 To see the close-button change its color, click on it without releasing
 the mouse-button

 The effect is quite unpredictable. I could not figure out a rule for which
 color comes up
 or which window needs how many clicks on resize to show the effect. But
 the above method
 worked quite reliably for me.
