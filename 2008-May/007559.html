 The back traces look very different. Please open separate tickets. The
 GobeProductive trace might just indicate a problem in the application
 itself (e.g. double free() or clobbered memory). The 0xffff0104 address is
 fine -- it is used for syscalls.

 Two hints:
  - gdb prints a line why a thread entered the debugger (among otherwise
 useless output). Please also provide it -- in doubt copy all output.
  - Please don't attach zipped text files unless there's a good reason
 (e.g. large test set or something). It just adds overhead for the people
 who look at the bug report, as they have to download and unzip the stuff
 first.
