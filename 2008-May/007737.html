Comment (by bonefish):

 Replying to [comment:10 axeld]:
 > I've investigated this a bit, and the Desktop window's message port is
 filled up with B_META_MIME_CHANGED messages - caused by the script that
 updates all MIME information upon first start.
 >
 > Not sure why it didn't manage to keep up. I think the solution to this
 problem in general should be to change the registrar to reporting in less
 detail when adding whole MIME types.

 Optimizing the MIME update message mechanism is certainly a good idea. I
 disagree, however, that this is the solution to this bug. That a port runs
 full should be rather harmless. That it isn't emptied is a real problem,
 though, and should be fixed. Until that happened, I wouldn't do anything
 that makes the bug less reproducible.
