Comment(by stippi):

 Contrary to BeOS, our VESA mode is actually pretty good, and running at
 32bpp will be the fastest. At 16bpp, there needs to be half the video
 memory to be written, but it also does a CPU intensive colorspace
 conversion. So unless you have an old computer with slow bus speed,
 running at 32bpp will be much faster.
