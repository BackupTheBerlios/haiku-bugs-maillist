Comment(by haiqu):

 Additional information for this package:

 1. It doesn't unpack under BeOS 5.1d0 (Dano) but I think this is probably
 a bug in the Expander.

 2. Instructions from the original package were included. They stated to
 add the following line to your UserBootscript:

 /home/config/bin/StatCacheServer &

 The correct command, which should be inserted in
 /boot/home/config/boot/UserBootscript is this:

 /boot/home/config/bin/StatCacheServer &

 Apologies for missing this error in the original installation document.
