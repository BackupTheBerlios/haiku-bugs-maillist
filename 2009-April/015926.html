Comment(by stpere):

 The bug was caused by the fact that the inset in the textview was
 calculated before adjusting the font size.  By enlarging the window, you
 didn't notice it because the font was following closely (it was calculated
 with the font from the previous iteration..) But at startup, it was
 calculating the inset using the small default font.

 This patch just move the font adjustment before the inset adjustment.

 Maybe I/we should convert this application to Layouts..
