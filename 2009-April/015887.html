 Further information on this ... I tried attaching a second monitor to see
 if the splash screen came up on it, not really expecting a result. What
 happened was that it did nothing at all. I checked resolutions and found
 that no widescreen modes are available.

 Then realizing the card isn't being detected at all and that I'm probably
 in some generic Vesa mode, I changed resolution from 1024 x 768 / 32 bit
 to 800 x 600 / 16 bit for faster response, and Hey Presto! now I get a
 splash screen on boot.

 Looks like the nVidia driver doesn't cover this card.
