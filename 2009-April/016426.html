Comment(by michael.weirauch):

 r30284; new-ata-bm; acpi

 PMCPU: CPU Power Management (throttling on inactivity)
 PMPCI: PCI BUS Power Management

 jam -qaj2 sessions:

  * BIOS: ~~EIST~~, ~~PMCPU~~, ~~PMPCI~~
    * freeze 12m14s
  * BIOS: ~~EIST~~, PMCPU, PMPCI
    * freeze 5m30s
  * BIOS: EIST, PMCPU, PMPCI
    * compile in 29m (finished after 30m30s uptime)
    * leaving system idle after compile: freeze after 32m30s uptime

 Perhaps the latest changes in bfs (r30221) or file_cache (r30276) do have
 some influence on the (greatly) improved uptimes. I wouldn't really say
 that enabling/disabling EIST, PMCPU or PMPCI - especially as when
 disabled, freezes appeared earlier - do have an influence. Perhaps just
 coincidence.

 Happy to report some success, though!
