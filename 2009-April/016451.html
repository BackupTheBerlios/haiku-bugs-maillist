 Haiku r30280

 Observation:

 Open a default size Terminal, execute a "seq 500", and click on the
 scrollbar above the scroll knob, so that the content scrolls one page up.
 The knob, though not minimally-sized, has moved less than its height.

 The same can be observed in Tracker. E.g. open /boot/system/bin and adjust
 the window height, so that the vertical scroll knob is a bit bigger than
 its minimum height.

 Expected behavior:

 As long as the knob has not been forced to its minimum height, scrolling
 by one page should move the knob by exactly its full height.

 I believe Stippi considers this a feature, but IMHO it's neither
 conventional nor consistent.
