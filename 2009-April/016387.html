 Rene and I were brainstorming a solution for defining targets that apply
 to more than one arch, but not all, and Rene suggested a "capabilities-
 based" system as suggested in the attached patch.

 Basically it defines each capability that may be arch-specific and uses
 that to determine whether it can be built or not.

 Currently OpenGL is builds for x86 and ppc, but not necessarily m68k or
 others. Likewise, libavcodec currently builds for x86, and *almost* builds
 for PPC, and probably will build for SPARC in the future as well.
