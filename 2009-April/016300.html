Comment(by stippi):

 Actually, I think this whole detaching/attaching of views which BTabView
 does is a design flaw of BeOS which we inherited (and it shows in exactly
 these situations). Unfortunately some applications may actually depend on
 it. But what we could do is change the behavior when layout-management is
 used. In that case we should use hiding/showing of the views. (Which may
 actually be the case already, now that I think of it...) In that case, the
 solution to this particular problem would be to convert Terminal to use
 layout-management.
