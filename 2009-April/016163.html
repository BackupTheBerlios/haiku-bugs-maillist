 As said in others ticket (I hope this won't be considered as duplicates,
 sorry if it is) there are some usability issues with DiskUsage. My patch
 fixes two of those :

 * "Jigger" when resizing the window

 * Zoom button not working (in appearance, I know it does something, but
 it's not obvious)

 So, basically, I just removed the proportion constraint on the window,
 which was working weird, and placed it on the pieView (to avoid having it
 clipped).

 Resizing is now smooth and the Zoom button zooms to the largest square
 window displayable, which I think is what it should do.

 I might tackle a few of the todos in that application.
