 I have a logical partition on my second HD called 'test' which I can mount
 with 'mountvolume test'.
 When I want to unmount it with mountvolume,
  - 'mountvolume -unmount test' gives "Volume 'test' not mounted."
  - 'mountvolume -unmount /test' gives "Failed to unmount volume '/test':
 Volume not found."
  - 'mountvolume -unmount /test/' gives "Failed to unmount volume '/test/':
 Volume not found."

 Under BeOS I can only unmount this volume with 'mountvolume -unmount
 /test', all the other options don't work.
