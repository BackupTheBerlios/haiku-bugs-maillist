 When trying to build to /dev/sda1, a 4.6GB partition, it aborts the
 process during 'creating the image' with an 'out of space' error.[[BR]]
 The only way I have found to trick it into succeeding is to first build a
 400MB haiku.image and then enabling HAIKU_DONT_CLEAR_IMAGE.[[BR]]
 Rev 30148, gcc2, on 32-bit Ubuntu 8.10.
