 I have a file that will be corrupt 9 times out of 10, after reboot.

 It's a 200Kb application cache, that may be written several times during a
 session.  I copy the file before I shut down.  On the next boot, the copy
 is fine, but the original is usually corrupt.  The invalid data is random
 blocks of data from other applications, commonly Firefox, and the boundary
 between valid and invalid data is at multiples of 4096.

 I'm at r28263, but it has been going on for a while.  I thought this might
 be interesting because it's so repeatable (on my computer), and so weirdly
 specific to this file.  I would be happy to add logging or gather other
 information that might be useful.
