#2960: Height of views change when adding/removing view

 When you add a view to ActivityMonitor, the height
   of the second view shrinks 1 pixel.
 When you add another view, the height
   of the first view shrinks 1 pixel,[[BR]]
   of the second view grows 1 pixel,[[BR]]
   of the third view shrinks 1 pixel.
 When you delete the two added views, the height
   of the first view grows 1 pixel.
 See 'ActivityMonitor (1 row legend).png'. (Also notice the drawing of the
 replicant-handles on top of their previous position.)[[BR]]
 [[BR]]
 [[BR]]
 When you add an extra row to the legend, things get really
 interesting:[[BR]]
 When you add a view to ActivityMonitor, the height
   of the first view grows 6 pixels,[[BR]]
   of the second view grows 5 pixels,
 When you delete the added view, the height
   of the second view grows 1 pixel.
 Doing this a couple of times enlarges the window noticeably. See
 'ActivityMonitor (2 row legend).png'.
