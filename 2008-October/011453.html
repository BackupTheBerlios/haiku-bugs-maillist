 Splitting this off from #1739 as it grew from the discussion.
 Freetype2 is part of Haiku, but the freetype2 headers, the freetype-config
 tool and its pkg-config files are missing.  This makes it difficult to
 port things to Haiku that rely on the system having those in place.  They
 don't need to be part of Haiku, but should at least be available as an
 optional package or part of the development package.
