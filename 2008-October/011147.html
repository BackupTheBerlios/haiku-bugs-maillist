 I used to have some iso images that i added to my vmware image and they
 got automatically mounted after boot. Since some revisions they won't show
 up in Trackers mount menu, but they are still shown e.g in DriveSetup (but
 impossible to mount). I've uploaded one of these images and see attached
 the part of my .vmx file.


 # ISO IMAGE
 ide0:1.present = "TRUE"
 ide0:1.fileName = "image.iso"
 ide0:1.deviceType = "cdrom-image"
 ide0:1.mode = "persistent"
 ide0:1.startConnected = "TRUE"
