 The ps2 driver publishes 4 mice when it is able to enable active
 multiplexing. The mouse needs to be reset initially. This is done when the
 device is opened. When no mouse is detected, an error is returned from
 open. You could first try to reset all mice ports from the ps2 driver, and
 then only publish those that are detected. However, because of hot plugin
 possibility, you still would have to reset the mouse when the individual
 device is opened. Two resets take longer than one reset, and timeouts are
 in the range of seconds.
