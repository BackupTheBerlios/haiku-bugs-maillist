Comment(by mmlr):

 This seems to be an uninitialized spinlock that is used inside the ide
 bus_manager. You should try out switching to the ata bus_manager instead,
 it shouldn't have this problem. To do that you'd go to src/add-
 ons/kernel/bus_managers/Jamfile and comment out the ide bus_manager and
 uncomment the ata one instead.
