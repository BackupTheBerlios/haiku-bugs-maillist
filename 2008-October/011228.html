 It's probably pretty pointless to have a midi player if you don't have the
 soundfonts to play the midi files. A lot of games depend on the midi kit.

 A decision will have to be made what soundfont could be used and included
 with Haiku. I'm not sure about licensing, or if there are any BSD/MIT
 licensed soundfonts, but I have found some freeware ones.

 I've tried a few archives, but could only get the midi player to play midi
 files with this one:

 [http://www.soundfonts.it/exec/zkdownload.pl?file=141-Compleet_bank__synth.zip]

 The file must be renamed 'big_synth.sy' and placed in /boot/beos/etc/synth
