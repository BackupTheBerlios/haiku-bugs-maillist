Comment(by stippi):

 You are driving a monitor at 1920x1200 via VGA?! The analog output of most
 graphics cards really sucks at such high resolutions. I would recommend
 getting a DVI KVM switch instead. Depending on the KVM, it may also screw
 up proper EDID retrieval.
