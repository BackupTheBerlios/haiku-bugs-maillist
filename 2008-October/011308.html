Comment(by axeld):

 Linux man pages are not a good source for these things, as they often have
 POSIX incompatible features that we might not always copy.

 http://www.opengroup.org/onlinepubs/009695399/functions/strftime.html is a
 better source of information, but it mentions %F as well :-)

 The build warnings are probably due to our old GCC version. '%+' doesn't
 seem to be in the standard, and you also seem to have used '%O' and '%E'
 incorrectly, as they are just modifiers and need another specifier.
