 It seems that some of the latest revisions (in the last week), have done
 something to the Vmware video driver. Under Vmware Fusion and build 23813,
 there are two Haiku screens (see pic).

 Removing the driver and using Vesa solves the problem, but the driver is
 much slower, and unstable (quitting BePDF will jump it into KDL).

 Interestingly, the problem can't be duplicated in Virtual Box, as the
 Vmware video driver works fine there.
