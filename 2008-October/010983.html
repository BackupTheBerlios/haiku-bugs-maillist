 I'd like to propose to either change the behaviour of Magnify's
 "Freeze/Unfreeze Image" function, or introduce one more function:

 but still do live updates of the screen area.
 It's useful when keeping an eye on a 16x16 icon while changing things in
 Icon-O-Matic for example or comparing render settings of the app_server
 etc.
