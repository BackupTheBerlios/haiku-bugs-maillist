Comment(by umccullough):

 FWIW, Standard Haiku builds don't boot properly on my G33-based ICH9
 machine either.

 When I switch the bus_manager from "ide" to "ata", however, it does boot
 fine (I don't have to fiddle with any BIOS modes either).

 If you're building your own images, try adding this to your
 UserBuildConfig:

 HAIKU_ATA_STACK = 1 ;

 mind the spacing, that's important.
