 This is r31031.

 The info section at the bottom of CharacterMap's window shows the
 character currently under the mouse pointer and offers its unicode values.
 Since here, the normal system font is used, some characters that are
 displayed in the character map of a selected font, are here only rendered
 as an outline box.

 I think, at least for the character itself, the same font as in the
 character map should be used.
