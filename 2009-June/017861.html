 On a ThinkPad 570E laptop running r30629, trying to play an MP3 through
 MediaPlayer produces no sound (though it appears to play and the VU meter
 is active).  The Deskbar Volume Control is entirely red, and the knob
 remains at '38dB' (it can be moved, but always returns to that value).
 I've tried reloading OpenSound -- even recompiling from current sources --
 with no effect.

 Under an earlier revision (29462) sound works... sometimes. (I understand
 this is a known bug, though I can't find it in the Tracker.)  The Volume
 slider works in this revision, though.

 The sound chip appears to be a Crystal CS4614, reported (in Media
 Preferences) as "CS4280" in the working version, and "CS461x" in the one
 that doesn't.
