Comment(by rudolfc):

 More info (to self):

 On NV34 it has become clear that after a failed modeswitch the pixelPLL
 isn't programmed (at least). Probably it's therefore not selected either.
 Which in turn means the card was setup for an old (pre) VGA style mode
 with fixed frequency setting.
 It's highly unlikely that any 'modern' VESA mode would be programmed
 without using the pixelPLL to determine the screen's refreshrate.
