 This is r32241.

 Normally, left-dragging a key copies it over the key you drop it, right-
 dragging exchanges the two keys (BTW: I'm for reversing the logic, as
 left-dragging is the more common action and exchanging is less
 destructive).

 Anyway, dragging a modifier key (SHIFT, COM, CTRL etc.) onto another
 /always/ copies, no matter what mouse button is used.

 Also, the whole upper row of keys (ESC, Fx-keys etc.) are neither copyable
 nor exchangeable.
