Comment:

 Committed a fix in r32501. I think the problem had something to do with
 the way the wrapping happened.  The text is put in a very small TextView
 (10x10), and with large fonts, we get this :

 T            <- a very tall line[[BR]]
 w            <- a short line[[BR]]
 o[[BR]]
 [[BR]]
 l[[BR]]
 i[[BR]]
 n[[BR]]
 e[[BR]]
 s[[BR]]

 And the size is only computed for the first 2 lines.  Then we resize the
 whole text view based on those stats.  What I did was to disable the
 WordWrap the time we get the size, then enable the WordWrap again.
