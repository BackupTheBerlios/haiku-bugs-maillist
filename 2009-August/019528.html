 Hi again,

 I just replaced the file, seems OK to me now.
 About the modeline use in the driver: as I said, the driver calculates the
 timing itself so it's the same percentage wise as the native modeline
 reported by the GPU. That is, this is the timing set in the CRTC. There's
 a translation layer which converts the CRTC timing to LVDS timing: the
 latter always runs in the native modeline as programmed by the GPU. The
 driver does not change that.
 In other words: the GPU performs modeline scaling for the panel.

 The story above is how it works for panels that are connected digitally.
 Analog connected screens (VGA style connection) really use the modeline in
 workspaces / the mode as set. As long as the driver determines there's no
 fault in it (like sync pulses outside the scope of a full line).

 I hope it's fully clear now..
