 This is a pretty old article about improving the Mac OS X Finder:

 http://arstechnica.com/apple/reviews/2003/04/finder.ars

 Despite the age it at least convinced me of usability of a spatial
 interface. It also has some interesting ideas, many of which Tracker and
 Haiku already do (full attribute support, saved queries) but also some
 others that are worth considering (a more separate browsing mode with more
 web browser-like features, or customizable label attributes that also
 affect the icons, view plug-ins.)

 I just wanted to leave a note concerning this now without distracting from
 the work on the alpha. Since Apple probably won't ever implement this in
 Finder, we can at least do it in Tracker, or consider some of the ideas.
