Comment(by mmlr):

 I think this was really caused by the tracing reattach. I've seen a
 similar crash once and it was when allocating tracing entries after
 changing something and rebooting (so after reattaching). Maybe we should
 disable reattaching by default and add a safemode setting in the
 bootloader since the feature is rarely used outside tracking specific
 bugs.
