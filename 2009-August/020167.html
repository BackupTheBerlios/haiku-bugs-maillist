 it looks like the ahci driver is working correctly. It detects 2 harddisks
 and one DVD-RW (see below).

 booting from sata. It is no longer maintained and replaced by the ata
 driver.

 As bug #1711 appears to be fixed, can you provide more information about
 booting with the ATA driver?
