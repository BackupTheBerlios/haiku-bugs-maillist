Comment(by jonas.kirilla):

 Wow. Kind of hard to see what just happened when you can't even 'ls' in an
 open Terminal.

 Could this be related to the proliferation of abandoned mountpoints at /?

 Disreali:

 What you describe happens at the root of the filesystem. (The volumes on
 your desktop are not symlinks.) R5 has the boot volume mounted at /boot,
 and a symlink to /boot by its name. Like this: /MyVolume (symlink) ->
 /boot (volume actually mounted here). I believe Haiku works the same here.

 Paths to files on the boot volume begin with "/boot/", as you can easily
 see in Tracker when opening the Info panel on files. This works the same
 on Haiku.
