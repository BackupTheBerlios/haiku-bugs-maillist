 This is r32748.

 Workspaces provides "special functionality", when left-clicking on a
 window-representation while holding a modifier key (CTRL, CTRL+SHIFT,
 OPT).

 When you try to use it, however, it switches workspaces if you click into
 another workspace pane.

 Holding one of those modifiers should disble workspace switching.
