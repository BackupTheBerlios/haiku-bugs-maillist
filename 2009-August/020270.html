 If you start the GLTeapot demo and then click with the mouse inside the
 teapot so that the teapot stops spinning, the window will also stop
 refreshing so if any part of the teapot is obscured, that part will
 disappear until you spin the teapot again. Moving the window itself also
 make the teapot disappear completely.

 Also, it may or may not be related but when the teapot is spinning and you
 move the window around, there is lots of flickering in the image.

 All of this is using the VESA driver and r32768.
