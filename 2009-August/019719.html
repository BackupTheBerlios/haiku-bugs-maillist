Comment(by axeld):

 The reasoning behind the current drag&drop behaviour is consistency:
 dropping a key from anywhere works in exactly the same way. Furthermore,
 exchanging keys isn't very obvious when you do it from different "planes"
 (ie. with and without modifier).

 To make it less destructive, the previous mapped character is copied to
 the preview text control.

 Anyway, that was my reasoning, I don't mean to say it's the ideal
 solution. Comments welcome, as always :-)
