 During startup, the graphical debugger spits out a ton of output when
 parsing the DWARF data in binaries. This frequently manages to completely
 deadlock Terminal (and consequently also the debugger, since it's stuck
 writing to stdout which the terminal isn't emptying buffers for),
 especially when run with a larger lib such as libtracker or libbe that's
 built with debugging information included.
