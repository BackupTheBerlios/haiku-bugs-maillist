 In drivers/disk/usb/usb_disk/usb_disk.cpp line 756 the current number of
 devices (gDeviceCount) determines the major part of the device name.

 The following scenario should results in duplicate device names, that hide
 a device.[[BR]]

 1) Connect USB disk A to PC -> disk/usb/0/...[[BR]]

 2) Connect USB disk B to PC -> disk/usb/1/...[[BR]]

 3) Remove USB disk A from  PC[[BR]]

 4) Connect USB disk A to PC -> disk/usb/1/... is reassigned
