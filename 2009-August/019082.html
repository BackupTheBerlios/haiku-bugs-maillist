 This is r32111.

 Currently there are these two items in the context menu: "No title" and
 "No Border". If one is chosen, a checkmark appears in front of it.

 Launchbox has a similar setting: "Show Window Border".

 I find the positive term including a verb much more attractive. I propose
 to change Workspaces behaviour accordingly to "Show Window Title" and
 "Show Window Border".
 Personally, I'm not sure a "Show Window Title" is even really necessary...

 Maybe this is something to include in the HIG?
