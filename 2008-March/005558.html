 Haiku currently uses MET and local time by default. Since you live in NZ,
 the time being off is just natural since you are also using Ubuntu which
 defaults to GMT.

 If you'd be using Windows, you wouldn't see a difference. While I am
 personally in favour of using GMT myself, probably most people are
 actually using Windows, and would then have exactly your problem. We had
 spoken about this on the mailing list some time ago, but we didn't come to
 any conclusion yet.
