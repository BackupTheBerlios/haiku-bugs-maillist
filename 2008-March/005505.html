 In Tracker, navigate to the artwork folder and select one of the png
 files. Use File->Open With enter the Open With window.

 The order of the candidate applications is backwards, the preferred
 application (ShowImage) should be the first item, not the last.

 Clicking on the Relation attribute title puts the list into the correct
 order.

 Tested in VMWare r24361
