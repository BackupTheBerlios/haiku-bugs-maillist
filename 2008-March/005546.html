Comment (by mmlr):

 Replying to [comment:4 bonefish]:
 > I don't see why heap_next_alloc_id() limits the IDs like that in the
 first place, but even if it used the full 16 bits, that would just make
 the situation less likely. Instead I'd use consecutive numbers for the
 pages of an allocation (starting with 1). I'll do that.

 It needs to limit the count because the allocation_id is in fact the
 free_count field, which is only 10 bits in size. But it is really a
 superfluous field anyway. You could use consecutive numbers in that field,
 or you could simply mark the last page of the allocation by setting the
 free_count to some specific value.
