 After a while of use in Haiku, the USB mouse freezes and the serial debug
 output shows:
 {{{
 Disabling unhandled io interrupt 11
 }}}
 The touch pad continues to work.

 Revision is r23895. Computer is a Lenovo Thinkpad T61 attached to a
 docking station. The mouse is connected to the USB port built-into the
 laptop (already before booting). When connected to the docking station USB
 ports it doesn't work at all. No other USB devices are attached. Unless
 I've overlooked it, there's no BIOS option to enable or disable USB legacy
 support (there's a "USB BIOS Support", which is enabled, but it is
 described as support for booting from USB disk/CD drive). IIRC, the
 attached serial debug output is from a session after a warm start.
