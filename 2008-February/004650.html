 To reproduce:

 Open the Email Preferences applet and create a "send and receive" email
 account type.

 Once done with entering all the Incoming and Outgoing mail settings, apply
 the settings and close the applet.

 Reopen the applet and click on Accounts > Outgoing. This will display the
 incoming email settings instead of the outgoing.
