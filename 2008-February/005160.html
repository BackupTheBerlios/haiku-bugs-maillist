 This bug is against the haiku trunk as of Thursday, Feb 21, 2008.  I am
 running on a Dell D600 Latitude laptop with a broadcom 570x chipset.

 02:00.0 Ethernet controller ![0200]: Broadcom Corporation NetXtreme
 BCM5705M Gigabit Ethernet [14e4:165d] (rev 01)

 lease, download firefox from bebits, etc.  But after a short (<10min)
 period of time, the driver hangs (generally hanging terminal too if I'm

 mouse works at first, and then fails.  If this appears to be related, or
