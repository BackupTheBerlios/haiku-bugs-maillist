#1859: Anti-aliasing issues with certain fonts
-----------------------+----------------------------------------------------
 Reporter:  j_freeman  |       Owner:  axeld         
     Type:  bug        |      Status:  new           
 Priority:  normal     |   Milestone:  R1            
Component:  - General  |     Version:  R1 development
 Platform:  All        |  
-----------------------+----------------------------------------------------
 Certain fonts are not anti-aliased when displayed at certain font sizes.
 For instance, Calibri and Cambria (of the [http://www.alkalay.net/software
 /webcore-fonts/webcore-fonts-3.0.tar.gz WebCore fonts] package) do not
 show up anti-aliased between sizes 13 and 17. You can test this in
 FontDemo.

 Also, in FontDemo when these fonts are displayed in that size range, if
 you uncheck the "Anti-aliased text" box, the text will disappear
 completely and will only be visible outside of that size range.

 Other fonts, such as Arial, do not have these problems.
