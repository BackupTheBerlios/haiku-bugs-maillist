 feather, clock and ProcessController visible and a too large, grey-only
 box below. It is not redrawn (time not updated) and does not respond to
 mouse input.

 This is mostly reproducible with SMP enabled. Haven't seen it with SMP
 disabled yet, nor with VM disabled.
