 On a system with 1GB of RAM, I successfully managed a complete checkout of
 the Haiku source tree from within Haiku in a single session. However, by
 the time it was done, there was ~90MB of RAM free, most of it being
 consumed by the kernel. A quick glance at listarea indicated around 3000
 block cache areas, so I'm wondering if this is possibly a leak. Leaving
 the sys alone for a while, doing a sync, etc. did nothing to decrease the
 mem usage or area count. This was on r23962.
