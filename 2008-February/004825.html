Comment (by cebif):

 Assuming you have a Mail account setup.
 More specific steps to reproduce this bug are:
 1) Right click on on the Deskbar Mail Daemon icon.
 2) Click on settings.
 3) Click apply button
 4) Close the settings window.
 5) Right click on Deskbar Mail Daemon and then go to folders and click on
 inbox subfolder.
 6) Double click on a message.
 Result is message will not open and the Tracker window cannot be closed.
 It is then necessary to kill tracker and restart the Desktop.
 After that right clicking on the Deskbar Mail Daemon and going to the
 inbox messages menu then clicking on a message will not open it. It is
 necessary to kill Mail that is still showing on the Deskbar. After that
 messages can be opened by right clicking on the Deskbar then clicking on a
 message in the Inbox menu.
