Comment (by kaliber):

 Tar 1.19 is newer and less annoying for me.

 Since 1.14(?) tar auto detects compression type:

 e.g with a new tar you can use:

 {{{
 tar xf test.tar.gz
 tar xf test.tar.bz2
 }}}


 with the current Haiku version you have to:

 {{{
 tar xfz test.tar.gz
 tar xfj test.tar.bz2
 }}}
