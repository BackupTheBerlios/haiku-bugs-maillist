 A couple days ago we assisted a visitor in #haiku in adding the ipro100
 driver to his Haiku image to support his Intel EtherExpress Pro 100
 (vendid: 8086 devid: 1229)

 He did confirm that after rebuilding the image to his partition that the
 netcard was detected and he was able to wget files.

 While not firsthand testing experience, I think this suggests that the
 driver does compile and at least function well enough to be included in
 the image by default.
