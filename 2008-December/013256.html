 This is r28814.

 The Options panel remembers its position from the last time. Is that
 really that useful? IMO it'd be better to open it at the position you've
 invoked it, i.e. somewhere near the DeskCalc application.

 DeskCalc is intelligent enough to ignore spaces and accept "," and "." as
 equal. How about
 ":" == "\" == "/" and
 "x" == "*"
 ?

 You can drag&drop a selection from the text field into Tracker, creating
 an "Untitled clipping" text file containing the dragged number. How about
 naming the file according to the dragged number or "DeskCalc - {number}".

 A small, inconsequential thing that's not even noticed by the average
 user, but kinda neat. Plus, someone who dragged a selection by accident
 can deduce at once what must have happened.
