 ''Sorry, I screw wiki formatting of this part:''

 For full double buffering, we'll needs '''two''' offscreen BBitmaps, one
 being the read-only visible frame, the second being the read-write
 (drawing) frame.
 Which leads to a triple buffers design:
  1. drawing buffer, where all directives are drawn
  2. display buffer, previous drawing buffer, painted to screen and used to
 repaint as needed
  3. screen frame buffer, eventually containing part (clipping) converted
 (color-spaces) copy of the display buffer.
