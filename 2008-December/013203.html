 This is r28814.

 The fast-forward/backward mode isn't easily stopped.

 Experienced behavior:
 Once you start fast-forwarding you can't go back to "play".
 You can press the fast-backward button to reverse direction, but you can't
 go back to "play".
 If you press "play", it pauses. If you press "play" again it goes back to
 fast-forwarding.
 You have to use the stop-button, which of course resets playback to the
 start of the CD.

 Expected behaviour:
 Either only fast forward as long as you press the button and revert to
 former mode (play/pause) on release.
 Or, one click starts fast-forwarding (highlight the arrows like with the
 play button) and another click on the ffwd-button or the play-button
 reverts to play.
