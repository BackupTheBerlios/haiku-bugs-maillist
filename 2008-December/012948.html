Comment(by umccullough):

 I suspect that eventually, this chipset will be supported by Haiku's HDA
 driver:

 http://dev.haiku-os.org/browser/haiku/trunk/src/add-
 ons/kernel/drivers/audio/hda

 But since that driver is somewhat incomplete still, and only supports a
 couple of chipsets, it might be a while.

 Have you tried the OpenSound optional package yet? You can add this to
 your haiku image by editing your UserBuildConfig and adding the line:

 AddOptionalHaikuImagePackages OpenSound ;

 It's not a perfect solution, but might at least give you sound.
