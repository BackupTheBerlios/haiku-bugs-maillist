 That PCIR_BAR(0) is correct after all. So either the card needs more
 configuring before being accessed through mmio (unlikely), or the mmio
 range isn't mapped into the proper virtual address.

 bus_alloc_mem_resource() allocates indifferently 128 kb. Looking at the
 listdev output we see that such an amount overlaps with the card reader's
 memory. It's a thin chance, but forcing the driver to allocate only the
 amount it needs may help.
