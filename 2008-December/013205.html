 This is r28814.

 I think a way to quickly jump to a certain track/title would be useful.
 Two approaches come to mind (the first looks a bit more complicated to
 me):

 1) An expander (the little triangle) under the control-buttons open a
 listview of the current CD contents with columns for track, title, time. A
 click on an entry jumps to that song and continues playing from there.

 2) Have a simple context menu activated when right-clicking in the view-
 box above the controls. The currently played song has a checkmark. Like in
 solution 1) we'll have the menu formatted in three columns: track, title,
 time.
