 On my Acer micro tower system, there was no functioning networking.  Upon
 discovery that the majority of the mainboard is based on the nForce
 chipset (which is supported in the Haiku driver set), it was realized that
 the issue was likely that the nForce driver hadn't been updated recently
 (there are several, more recent, nForce chipsets that are not in the
 current driver).

 Since then, Tonestone made an updated nForce driver binary available to me
 (which works fine, so far as I've used it), but it was suggested by Urias,
 I report a ticket here, to make sure it is properly addressed/handled by
 the devs.
