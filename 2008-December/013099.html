 Steps to reproduce:

 1) open workspaces applet.[[BR]]
 2) Set up two workspaces of differing resolutions, i.e. workspace 1 @
 1280x1024 and workspace 2 @ 800x600.[[BR]]
 3) Move window to top left corner in workspace 1.[[BR]]
 4) Switch to workspace 2.[[BR]]
 5) Move window down a few hundred px[[BR]]
 6) Switch back to workspace 1 - the window will have moved far more than
 you moved it in workspace 2.[[BR]]
 Continuing to switch back and forth sometimes provokes other random window
 movements. Note that this seems to work fine when the workspace
 resolutions are the same, and I've been unable to find out the reason why
 the resolution switch makes a difference.[[BR]]
